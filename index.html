About Me

I began my journey as a web developer in the spring of 2017, after a chance encounter with a founder of a coding bootcamp in Utah. After the local coding school closed, and after speaking with former graduates, I decided to begin a self taught curriculum that has been as exciting as it is challenging.

My skills are well rounded, from front end to back end development. I enjoy each aspect, and love building sites from start to finish.

When not online, I can be found in Las Vegas, NV with my wife, Kristle - trying out a new recipe in the kitchen, planning our next part of the world to explore, or enjoying a leisurely round of golf.
